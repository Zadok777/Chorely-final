# Chorely Support

Need help with Chorely? You're in the right place.

## Contact us

**Email:** doulosnexus@gmail.com
We aim to respond within **2 business days**.

When you write in, it helps to include:
- the email address on your Chorely account,
- your device (iPhone/iPad or Android) and app version (shown at the bottom of **More → Help center**), and
- a short description of what happened.

## Common topics

### Getting started
- **Add a child:** open **Family** and tap **+**. Children are profiles you manage — they don't sign in.
- **Create and assign a chore:** use **Chores** or a Home quick action, set a point value, and assign it to a child.
- **Approve a chore:** when a chore is done, approve it to award the points.
- **Rewards:** create rewards with a point cost in the **Reward catalog**, then redeem them for a child once they have enough points.

### Account
- **Edit your profile or family name:** **More** → tap your avatar, or use the Family name row.
- **Delete your account:** **More → Delete account.** See the [Account Deletion](./account-deletion.md) page for what's removed.

### Subscriptions & billing
- **Chorely Plus** unlocks unlimited children and chores plus premium features, billed monthly or yearly through Apple or Google.
- **Manage or cancel:** in your device's **App Store** (Apple ID → Subscriptions) or **Google Play** (Payments & subscriptions) settings.
- **Restore a purchase:** use **Restore Purchases** after signing in with the same Apple or Google account you bought it with.

### Privacy & safety
- Chorely is **parent-managed**; children never sign in or enter personal information.
- See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md) and [Terms of Use](./terms-of-use.md).

## In-app help

Open **More → Help center** in the app for these answers any time, plus a one-tap email link.
